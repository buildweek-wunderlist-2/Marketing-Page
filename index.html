Features

Create

Create organized to-do lists to keep your personal and professional life in order. Choose from a variety of ready-to-use templates, or create your own custom lists.

Schedule

Set reminders for time-sensitive and important tasks. Schedule recurring events for frequently used lists and projects. Automatically sync your lists with your favorite calendar.

Collaborate

Comment and collaborate on shared lists with friends and colleagues. Add contributors to your lists to see receives updates and notifications in realtime.

Share

Share your lists with your network on social media. See a list from a friend or follower that you like? Automatically save it to your favorites, or create a copy and start using it right away.

About

Wunderlist 2.0 takes your traditional to-do list app and automates it. It not only allows you to write your to-do, work, grocery, trip and household lists, but allows you to set up recurring to do lists by date and time. (Think recurring events on google calendar + to-do list app). No matter how much is on your plate, Wunderlist 2.0 makes it super easy to remember all the little recurring to-dos and surprise to-dos that pop up unexpectedly.
